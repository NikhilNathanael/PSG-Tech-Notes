# Inventory Management

`=[[]].definition`

- Inventory management involves
  - Planning
  - Coordinating and controlling acquisition
  - Storage
  - Handling movement
  - Moment, distribution and possible sales of 
    - Raw materials
    - Component parts
    - Subassemblies
    - Supplies and tools
    - Replacement parts
  and other assets that are needed to meet customer wants and needs
